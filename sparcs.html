Star-Planet Activity Research CubeSat

Mission Goal

A team of graduate researchers and ASU staff are working to develop a small space telescope with the mission of monitoring the flares and sunspot activity of M-type stars. SPARCS will be ready for launch by the end of 2023 to a Sun-synchronous orbit.

My Part in This

I'm responsible for designing a jig used to test optics that will be used in the CubeSat. The components I modeled and drafted were machined professionally by the ASU Machine and Electrical Shop.

In the near future, I will assist in in ISO 3 cleanroom maintenance as well as the preparation / execution of experiments meant to test other hardware to be used in the spacecraft.

Through this experience, I've gained insight in the practical applications and limits to CAD modelling and drafting, as well as important practices to adhere to when designing parts.
